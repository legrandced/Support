# Privacy Policy

Effective as of 01/04/2026

## Article 1 — Data Controller
Cédric Legrand, acting as the publisher of the application "My Wine Cellar", is the controller of your personal data. It attaches great importance to the secure, transparent, and confidential collection and processing of your data, in accordance with Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR).

You can contact him at the following address: info@macaveavins.app.

## Article 2 — Data Collected and Purposes
We only collect data strictly necessary for the provision and improvement of our services:
- **Identification data**: Your email address is required to create and secure your account.
- **Content data**: You can add information about your wines, including photographs of labels, which are essential to the main service of the Application.
- **Transaction data**: Information relating to Premium subscriptions is processed securely by the Apple App Store and the Google Play Store. In-app purchase events are also transmitted to Meta Platforms (Facebook) via the Meta SDK integrated into the Application, in order to measure the performance of our advertising campaigns.
- **Technical data**: We collect anonymized information about your device and your version of the application to ensure the maintenance and improvement of the service.
- **Behavioral data**: We collect aggregated usage data via an analytics service (see Article 3). Certain key actions in the Application are also transmitted to Meta Platforms for advertising purposes (see Article 3).

The processing of this data is based on the performance of the contract binding us (provision of the service) and, for data transmitted to Meta, on your consent collected via the tracking authorization request (App Tracking Transparency on iOS). On Android, this processing is based on our legitimate interest in measuring the effectiveness of our advertising.

## Article 3 — Data Sharing and Transfers
We do not sell or rent your personal data. They may be shared with subcontractors for specific technical needs:
- **Third-party AI service providers**: Label photographs are transmitted to specialized providers to automatically extract wine information. These transfers are secure. The list of relevant providers is available upon request at info@macaveavins.app.
- **Hosting and analytics services (Google Firebase)**: Your data is stored and your aggregated usage data is analyzed via Firebase. For more information: [https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy).
- **Meta Platforms (Facebook)**: The Application integrates the Meta App Events SDK. Events related to key actions in the Application (registration, viewing the Premium offer, use of the scan feature, in-app purchases) are transmitted to Meta to measure the performance of our ads and optimize our campaigns. This data may include your advertising identifier, subject to your consent. For more information: [https://www.facebook.com/privacy/policy](https://www.facebook.com/privacy/policy).

**Regarding transfers outside the EU**: Firebase and Meta Platforms are American companies. These transfers are governed by the European Commission's standard contractual clauses (SCCs) and, where applicable, the EU-US Data Privacy Framework.

## Article 4 — Retention Period
Your personal data is kept as long as your account is active. In case of prolonged inactivity for a period of five years, your data will be deleted. You can also request the deletion of your data at any time via the Application settings.

## Article 5 — Consent and advertising tracking control

**On iOS**: Upon your first use of the Application, an advertising tracking authorization request (App Tracking Transparency) is presented to you. If you decline, your advertising identifier will not be transmitted to Meta and advertising tracking will be disabled. You can change this choice at any time in your device settings: *Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking*.

**On Android**: Advertising tracking is active by default, in accordance with standard platform practices. You can opt out in your device settings: *Settings > Privacy > Ads*.

## Article 6 — Your Rights
In accordance with the GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, limit the processing, and port your data. To exercise these rights, contact us at info@macaveavins.app.

In the event of a dispute, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Data Protection Authority in Belgium.

## Article 7 — Security
We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk, including encryption and pseudonymization where possible. All data is transmitted via encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS).

## Article 8 — Modifications
This privacy policy may be updated. The User will be informed of any substantial changes by email or via a notification in the Application.
